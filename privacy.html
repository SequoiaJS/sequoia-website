Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

Sequoia Automatic, Inc. d/b/a Sequoia Precision Components ("Sequoia," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal information through our website at sequoiaweb.com (the "Site").

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through our Request a Quote (RFQ) form, including:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Company name
  • Target market selection
  • Project details and component requirements

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics services, or other automated data collection technologies on this Site. We do not collect information automatically from your browser or device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your quote request or inquiry
  • Communicate with you about your project requirements
  • Provide engineering and sales support related to your inquiry

We do not use your personal information for marketing purposes unless you separately consent to receive marketing communications.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only:

  • With our affiliated manufacturing facilities (Saltillo, Mexico; Nanjing, China; Chonburi, Thailand) as necessary to respond to your inquiry
  • With service providers who assist us in operating our business, subject to confidentiality obligations
  • As required by law, regulation, or legal process

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, maintain our business relationship, and comply with legal obligations. Quote inquiry data is typically retained for three (3) years.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know: Request details about the personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: Request that we delete your personal information.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt Out: We do not sell or share personal information for targeted advertising. If this changes, we will provide an opt-out mechanism.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below. We will respond to verifiable requests within 45 days.

7. California Residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information as described in Section 6 above. In the preceding 12 months, we have collected the categories of personal information listed in Section 1. We do not sell personal information and have not done so in the preceding 12 months.

8. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
