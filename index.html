Our Story

One Standard.
Every Facility.
Every Component.

Sequoia Automatic was founded in 1958 as a screw machine shop in Crystal Lake, Illinois. Over six decades, we evolved into a global precision machining leader specializing in aluminum thermal management components.

Our strategic partnership with the SV Group — now a full merger after 20 years of collaboration — expanded our manufacturing footprint first to Jiangsu, China and later to Chonburi, Thailand. The establishment of Sequoia Precision Components in Saltillo, Mexico positions us to serve the rapidly growing North American nearshoring market.

Today, we serve virtually every global Tier-1 and Tier-2 thermal management supplier in the automotive industry, and we're bringing that same precision to the data center liquid cooling revolution.

1958 Crystal Lake, IL — Founded as a precision screw machine shop
2005 SV Group Partnership — Expanded to Nanjing, Jiangsu, China
2015 Saltillo, Mexico — Sequoia Precision Components for USMCA nearshoring
2016 Chonburi, Thailand — SV Group expansion into Southeast Asia
2025 Full Merger — One unified organization across four facilities, three continents
