Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

Sequoia Automatic, Inc. d/b/a Sequoia Precision Components is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

Our Efforts

We have taken the following steps to ensure accessibility:

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper headings, landmarks, and labels
  • Keyboard navigability throughout the site
  • Descriptive alt text for all images
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios for text content
  • Visible focus indicators for interactive elements
  • Accessible forms with proper labels and required field indicators
  • Respect for user motion preferences via prefers-reduced-motion

Known Limitations

While we strive for comprehensive accessibility, some areas may still be in progress. We are continuously working to improve.

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our website, we want to hear from you. Please contact us:

Email: info@sequoiaweb.com
Subject line: Website Accessibility

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within five (5) business days and to resolve reported issues as quickly as possible.

Standards

This website aims to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA, the standard referenced by the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and the DOJ's Title II web accessibility rule.
